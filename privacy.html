Privacy Policy for SmsGuard

Last updated: 2026

SmsGuard respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

SMS Messages

SmsGuard accesses SMS messages only to provide messaging functionality such as sending, receiving, spam detection and message management.

All processing happens locally on your device.

SmsGuard does NOT upload message content to any server.

Phone Numbers

Phone numbers may be used for spam detection and blocking.

If you report a number as spam, only the phone number may be sent anonymously.

Message content is never transmitted.

Data Storage

All data including messages, blocked numbers and spam rules are stored locally on your device.

Permissions

SmsGuard may request SMS permissions to send, receive and manage messages.

Advertising

The app may display ads via Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers according to their privacy policy.
